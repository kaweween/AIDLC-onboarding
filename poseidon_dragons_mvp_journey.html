The problemSlide 2 of 10
Everything was manual
Before this project, the team ran operations across three disconnected tools.
Messenger
Training schedules, member polls, announcements, payment screenshots — all in a group chat with no structure
Google Sheets
Member roster maintained manually by officers. No validation, no access control, anyone with the link could edit
Google Drive
Payment screenshots, ID photos, and documents stored in folders with no link to member records
No audit trail
No history of who changed what, no payment confirmation system, no attendance records beyond chat messages
"Officers had to manually update three different places every time anything changed about a member."

What we builtSlide 6 of 10

Team info, benefits, training schedule, Google Maps, membership application form with photo upload
Officer login

Key correctionsSlide 7 of 10
The human in the loop
Every correction Kareen made became a documented decision. This is what made AIDLC work — not just accepting AI output.
  • K
    Tailwind v4 broke the build — Kareen caught it immediately. Pinned to v3. Now documented in specs.
  • K
    Role mismatch — Kareen's Firestore role was team_manager, not head_officer. Fixed without touching production data.
  • K
    Dragonboat positions — Generic labels corrected to actual positions: Bow/Left, Stroke/Right, Ambidextrous, Drummer, Helm.
  • K
    Login freeze — navigate() during render caused infinite loop. Fixed with useEffect pattern.
  • K
    Import status mapping — All members imported as pending. Kareen caught it; added Membership Status column priority and freeze/resign → inactive mapping.
  • K
    QR codes — Requested both BPI and GCash QR codes, not just BPI. Small detail, big UX difference.

Rollout planSlide 9 of 10
Four phases — officers first, members second
Designed to replace existing workflows gradually, not all at once.
A
Now — 2 weeks
Officer data entry
Bulk import members, record 2026 payments, backfill attendance, verify all profiles. App becomes single source of truth.
B
Weeks 3–4
Officer full operation
Training schedule in app, Messenger post generator replaces manual typing, payments and attendance fully live. Test with 2–3 trusted members.
C
After Phase 2 Priority 3
Member soft launch
Member login goes live. Announce in GC. Members verify own profiles, use digital ID, QR check-in at training.
D
Full rollout
App is the primary channel
Announcements replace Messenger for official posts. Members sign up for training in app. Digital ID used for race registration.
